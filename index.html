DARSHA GLOBAL CONSTRUCTIONS LTD

Over 17 Years Experience

DARSHA GLOBAL CONSTRUCTIONS LTD is a self-motivated, home-grown company with its head office located in Abuja, Nigeria. Our highly mobile workforce is capable of rapidly deploying to project sites across the country and beyond . With a team of skilled engineers from diverse professional backgrounds, we are well-equipped to handle challenging projects and deliver exceptional results. Over the years, DARSHA GLOBAL CONSTRUCTIONS LTD, in collaboration with our technical partners, has successfully designed and executed a range of construction projects. Some of our notable projects include:

  • Construction of the Dangote Student Hostel at Ahmadu Bello University (ABU), Zaria.
  • Construction of student common rooms and associated external works at ABU Zaria.
  • Completion of the Voice of Nigeria (VON) Head Office.
  • Various privately owned residential buildings in Abuja, among others.

DARSHA GLOBAL CONSTRUCTIONS LTD has an outstanding record of operation with its financial partners. The asset base of our directors ensures that we can finance and execute projects efficiently without any financial constraints.
